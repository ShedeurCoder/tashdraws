TERMS OF SERVICE:

⩥ I reserve the right to refuse any commission request.
⩥ Commissions are done on a first come, first serve basis. If you would like a drawing done by a certain deadline, please let me know so we can work something out.
⩥ Please allow me 1-4 weeks to complete the commission.
⩥ Payment via Paypal, or Gcash (local PH clients)
⩥ 100% payment upfront OR at least 50% payment upfront, then remaining amount after sketch is approved.
⩥ For personal and non-commercial use only! (profile picture, wallpaper, banner, print out and frame, gift, etc). Please do not claim it as your own, and do not use it for profit. I retain all copyrights over the commissioned artwork.
⩥ No refunds or returns once I have started working on the commission.
⩥ If upfront payment has not been made one week after I approved your commission request, I will cancel your slot and proceed to the next in line.
⩥ If you wish to make changes on anything that has already been approved, an additional fee will be charged.
⩥ Do not use the commissioned art with/as NFTs and AI
⩥ I may post the commissioned drawing on my social media or include it in a portfolio. However, if you don't want me to post it, please let me know

PROCESS:

⩥ DM me if interested or if you have any questions! Specify commission type (bust/half body/full body, sketch/full color), how many characters, and any necessary details and information. Also provide an email address where I can send the finished comm.
⩥ Go to the Google form here and submit the details
⩥ Please provide as many references as you can (photos, written descriptions, pose reference, your own drawings, etc) so I can make the drawing as accurate as possible
⩥ I will send a confirmation email once upfront payment has been made and sketch phase has begun. No refunds at this time
⩥ Sketch will be sent for approval. Minor changes/revisions are allowed at this stage. Then color palette/thumbnail will be sent for approval. I will be sending regular updates on your commission
⩥ Finished artwork in full resolution will be emailed to you in PNG format
